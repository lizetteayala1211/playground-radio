YUNG BUGARRON

As a multidisciplinary artist, his mixes explore a wide range of influences from the QTPOC NYC dance scene to the creative works he’s synthesized mirroring his experiences growing up.

UNPACKING WORLD
A raw conversation every episode, with voices from the service industries, health and wellness fields, local politics, and beyond.

Topics range from tipping culture, racism, equitable and living wage, to stories about the workers, caregivers, and regulars that make up New York City.

Hosted by Tinuade Oyelowo, with music by danymane. Produced by Tinuade Oyelowo, John Barrington, and Dany Brito.
CY X
Cy X is storyteller and cyber witch merging sound, video art, performance, and sonic-based research.

Their practice is grounded in the art of synthesis: truth generation and sound generation which is used to create portals to explore new possibilities.

Fusing art, sound, and technology with the practice of witchcraft, they are inspired to use spells, rituals, and alchemic practices to fundamentally alter the world around us.
TRAVIS BROWN
Travis Brown is a multidisciplinary creative from Largo, MD who’s been involved with various radio platforms since 2010.

As a culmination of jazz training and a deep love for music, ‘By Proxy’ is Travis’s vehicle to share musical and emotional exploration within varying genres.

Aside from radio, Travis hosts a vinyl show on Twitch (“Travis Plays…”), freelances on work varying from graphic design to event planning, and enjoys conversations amongst friends old and new.

EDICA+
“DUB & EFFECTION” (D&E) is a radio show and online platform dedicated to Soundsystem Culture and Dub music, including its relative electronic subgenres (bass, grime, reggae, hip hop, ambient, downtempo, instrumental, remix, etc).

D&E also spotlights the art and profiles of creatives around the world whose work is influenced by the Caribbean's "heavyweight champion sound".

By educating listeners on the history of Dub and its deep roots in much of today’s popular music, D&E is a "salute to the revolutionary foundation of modern sound."

D&E is hosted by its founder EDICA+, a Guyanese Brooklyn-based DJ, Sound Designer, Curator, and Dub Artist. In addition to her selections of eclectic global sounds, she goes far beyond as she takes her audience on trippy, otherworldly sonic journeys.

She was featured as a top 10 DJ to follow by ESSENCE Magazine and continuously evolves her artistry with new experimental audio+visual projects.

Her explorative sound trips are an experience to catch, as she dives deep into classic yet refreshing vibrations that simultaneously bump the body and massage the mind.

DINNER WITH US SERIES
Dinner With Us Series - In the summer of 2019 Playground launched “Dinner With Us”, a community dinner series.

We introduced the initiative through a three course meal (made by materials and produce donated by local food organizations, farms and CSAs) while highlighting the detrimental food climate that exists in Bed-Stuy.

The access to healthy and sufficient food sources are limited in our community. Our intention is to start a conversation and introduce practices in food security and solution based thinking.

For this iteration we will shift our focus to see how we produce high levels of food waste when the people in our community are food insecure. Our mission is to provide insight about food waste at a consumer and community level.
